osu!

osu! is a popular free-to-play rhythm game where players click circles, slide over tracks, and spin objects to the beat of a soundtrack. It features four distinct game modes
(osu!standard, osu!taiko, osu!catch, & osu!mania ), with the most popular being osu!standard. It also has customizable difficulty beatmaps, and a global community that
creates and shares thousands of user-made songs.
